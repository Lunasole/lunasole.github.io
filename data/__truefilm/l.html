              Recent update: 06/02/2019


              This page holds all the films and full-length anime titles I watched for all time. To be exact, those of them that I mark as "valuable and noticeable" or even "epic, unique".
              I've started this list few years ago, mainly "to not forget what I ever watched", as soon as I realized how unstable and inaccurate becomes human associative memory over a long time :)

              For now, the list is large enough and represents almost all films I ever liked. It doesn't helps a lot to not forget every noticeable film, but well, for this purpose such a list is much better than nothing, and as for me more better/practical than any service for that.

              "So what?"
              Generally I don't know why you should be interested to view this list ^_^ Maybe just some of following cases: you absolutely don't know what movie you'd like to watch today, or you want to learn my preferences a bit and somehow compare with your own.
              Anyway it is here and is synchronized time from time with offline version.

              PS. Most of the titles below using localized names (ru), as well as genre tags. I can easily convert all the list to EN, but for what? (for me it is more practical to keep mixed langs).Also, don't be surprized that it looks unsorted — the titles are sorted by date added to a list, so yes, it looks so :)
